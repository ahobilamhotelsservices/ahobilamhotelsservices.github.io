04 The Sacred Trek

From the Upper Temple, the trek begins. Follow the path carefully.

A. Kroda (Varaha) Narasimha

Located very close to the main Upper Ahobilam temple. The Lord is in the form of a Boar.

Varaha Temple
Lord Kroda

Route Diversion at Varaha Temple:

Option A: Malola Path (Right)

Take steps on Temple Right. Climb 250 steps (30 mins). Easy route.

Option B: Jwala Path (Left)

Take Bridge on Left. River route + 900 steps (1.5 Hours). Scenic route.

Tip: Go to Jwala (Left) first -> Cross top bridge -> Come down via Malola (Right).

B. Jwala & Malola

Proceed further to reach Jwala (where the Lord killed Hiranyakashipu) and Malola (Lord with Lakshmi).

Way to Jwala
Malola Narasimha
