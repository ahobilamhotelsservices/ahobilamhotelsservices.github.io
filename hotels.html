FAQs - Accommodation in Ahobilam

1. Which types of accommodations are available in Ahobilam?
Ahobilam offers various stay options including Private Guest Houses, APTDC Haritha Hotel, Ahobila Mutt Chatrams, and budget lodges.
2. Is it necessary to book rooms in advance?
Yes, absolutely. During weekends, Swathi Nakshatram days, and festivals like Brahmotsavams, rooms get fully booked very fast.
3. What are the check-in and check-out timings?
Most private guest houses offer a 24-hour check-in/check-out policy. However, government hotels like Haritha have fixed timings (10 AM Check-in, 9 AM Check-out).
4. Are food facilities available at these hotels?
Hotels like Haritha have attached restaurants. For others, there are many private canteens and mess facilities available near the temple.
5. Is car parking available?
Yes, almost all guest houses and hotels in Ahobilam provide safe parking spaces for cars and tourist buses.

Our mission is to provide excellent accommodation and service to the devotees visiting the holy abode of Ahobilam Narasimha Swamy.
