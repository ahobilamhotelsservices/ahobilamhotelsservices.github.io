Frequently Asked Questions

Common doubts clarified for pilgrims

Is there mobile signal in Ahobilam?
Yes, Jio and Airtel have good coverage in Lower Ahobilam. In Upper Ahobilam and trekking routes, the signal might be weak or unavailable. BSNL also works in some areas.
Are food facilities available?
Yes, free Annadhanam (meals) is provided by Arya Vysya and Brahmana Satrams in the afternoon (12:00 PM - 2:00 PM). For dinner, small hotels and tiffin centers are available in Lower Ahobilam.
What are the restrictions for room booking?
Since Ahobilam is a holy Kshetram:
  • Alcohol and Smoking are strictly prohibited.
  • Non-Vegetarian food is not allowed in rooms.
  • Unmarried couples are not allowed to book rooms.
  • Valid Government ID proof (Aadhar) is mandatory.
Is hot water available in rooms?
Most Satrams provide hot water only in the morning hours (5:00 AM to 8:00 AM). Private guest houses usually have Geysers available 24/7. Please check the specific amenity list while booking.
How to reach Upper Ahobilam from Lower?
Upper Ahobilam is 8 KM from Lower Ahobilam. You can go by your own vehicle, take a shared auto, or use the local bus service. The road is good and scenic.
